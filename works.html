What is SpacedR?

SpacedR stands for Spaced Repetition.

Spaced repetition is a method of reviewing material at systematic intervals.
At the beginning of the learning process, the intervals are spaced closely together
(for example, one hour; four hours; one day).
As the material is reviewed, the intervals become systematically longer
(for example: four days; one week; two weeks).
